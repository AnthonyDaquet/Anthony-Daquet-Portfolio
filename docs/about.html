ABOUT


Hi! I'm Anthony.


Thanks for checking out my portfolio! My passion for graphic design started in early high school and I've been practicing it ever since. I've been a professional Graphic Designer for about 6 years. I've worked at various places such as Canon Solutions America, Minuteman Press and interned at Marvel Entertainment. I currently work at Professional Retail Services and its sister companies, Retail Security Services and Retail Mechanical Services. I design for their social media, websites and their marketing materials.

Aside from graphic design, I am obsessed with anything superhero related from comics to TV shows and movies. I also enjoy reading manga, playing video games, Dungeons & Dragons and cooking.
